Copycat Din Tai Fung Cucumber Salad

Description

This Copycat Din Tai Fung Cucumber Salad is bursting with flavor. Beautifully marinated cucumbers drizzled in chili oil (optional) is an outstanding vegetarian appetizer you can enjoy for any special occasion!

Ingredients

  • 8 cucumbers
  • 1 and a half tablespoons of kosher salt
  • 3 Tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 Tablespoons sugar
  • 1 and a half tablespoons Mirin
  • 1 and a half tablespoons sesame oil
  • 3 cloves garlic grated or minced
  • half teaspoon kosher salt

Instructions

  1. Slice cucumbers into 1/2 thick pieces
  2. Add sliced cucumber to a small bowl. Sprinkle with 1 and 1/2 TBSP kosher salt and toss to combine. Let sit 30 minutes in the refrigerator
  3. After 30 minutes, rinse off the salt mixture and pat the cucumbers dry.
  4. Meanwhile, make the dressing. in a small bowl whisk together the rice vinegar, mirin, sugar, sesame oil, garlic and salt. Mix well and set aside.
  5. Toss the rinsed and dried cucumbers with the dressing and let marinate for minimum of 4 hours, up to 2 days.
  6. To serve: Taste for seasoning, and add additional salt if needed. Drizzle with chili oil (optional) and sprinkle with fresno chili (optional) and serve chilled.
